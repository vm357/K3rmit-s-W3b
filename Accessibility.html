NON-ACCESSIBLE CONTENT

Below is a description of known limitations we're working to address.
1. Color contrast in some design elements: Some decorative elements and accent colors may not yet meet the recommended WCAG 2.2 Level AA contrast ratios on all devices and screen settings.
2. Keyboard navigation review in progress: Not all interactive elements have been fully tested for keyboard-only navigation and focus visibility.
3. Alternative text for future portfolio images: Some portfolio or project images added in the future may require additional descriptive alternative text to ensure accessibility for screen reader users.
4. Third-party integrations: Accessibility of embedded third-party tools, forms, or widgets may vary and is dependent on the accessibility practices of those providers.
We're prioritizing these fixes with completion expected by Kermit Webcraft is committed to continuously improving accessibility and usability. We regularly review our website and services to identify and address accessibility barriers..
